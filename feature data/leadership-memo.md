# Leadership memo — Q3 priority input
**From:** Rob Callahan, CEO  
**To:** Product team  
**Date:** July 8, 2025  
**Re:** Enterprise reporting — priority for Q3

---

I want to flag a conversation from last week that needs to shape our Q3 roadmap discussion.

I met with the executive sponsor at Meridian Group — our largest account at $340K ARR — and the topic of reporting came up directly. Their VP of Operations asked whether Terraloom can produce a cross-project status report that shows drawing version history, approval status, and overdue tasks in a single exportable view. Right now their PMs are pulling this together manually in Excel every Friday for their internal leadership meeting.

They're up for renewal in 45 days. The renewal isn't at risk today, but the VP made it clear that if this capability exists in a competitor tool they evaluate, it would be a meaningful factor. I don't want to lose this account over a report.

Here is what I am asking for:

A cross-project reporting dashboard that allows project managers to see drawing publication status, approval queue, and overdue task count across all active projects in one view, with CSV export. This doesn't need to be beautiful — it needs to exist.

I know we have competing priorities and I trust the team to make the right call. But I want to be transparent: this is a real commercial pressure with a real deadline, and I would like to understand how we're thinking about it before the end of the week.

Happy to join the roadmap discussion if it's useful.

— Rob
